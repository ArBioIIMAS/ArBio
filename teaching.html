Artificial Inteligence in Biomedicine (ArBio group)
Teaching

Our research areas are focused in: image processing, computer vision and machine learning and deep learning. In particular, our published works cover medical imaging (Rx, CT, MRI, PET), neuroimaging (MRI, DTI, fMRI) and photomicrography. Also, we have addressed topics such as segmentation and detection, video tracking, underwater monitoring, 3D reconstruction and anomaly detection. In addition, we have worked with supervised, unsupervised and reinforced classification.
