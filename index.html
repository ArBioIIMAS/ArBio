Mission

Artificial Inteligence in Biomedicine Group (ArBio)

ArBio is a multi-disciplinary research group interested in applied artificial intelligence to advance biomedicine areas. The group was created in response to current demands related to the development and use of image data analysis and pattern recognition methods in 2019. Our solutions cover the areas of medicine, biology, cognitive and/or environmental sciences, so on. ArBio belong to the Unidad Académica del Instituto de Investigaciones en Matemáticas Aplicadas y en Sistemas en el estado de Yucatán UAEY - at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México.

  • Our interest areas are:

  • Computer vision

  • Stereoscopic vision and 3D reconstruction

  • Biomedical images and data analysis

  • Machine and deep learning algorithms

  • Are welcome:

  • PhD students

  • Master's students

  • Internships

  • Teaching

Our mission

Mission

Create new technology and knowledge in the area of image processing, computer vision, data analysis and pattern recognition. As well as train high-level graduate students in these areas.

Vision

Implement robust computational algorithms to solve current problems at a regional, national, and international level for solving real biomedicine problems.
